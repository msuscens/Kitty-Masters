Welcome to Mark's Crypto-Kitties

Collectable digital cats that you can uniquely own, breed, buy and sell!

How to get started:


1. Connect your metamask wallet

2. Buy at least two kitties (in the Kitty-Market)

3. View your kitties (in My Kitty-Pen)

4. Breed your kitties to create offspring (new unique kitties)

5. Advertise your kitties for sale (or simply collect them)

6. Receive payment automatically when your kitty is sold


Note: The keys to your kitties are held in your metamask wallet.
